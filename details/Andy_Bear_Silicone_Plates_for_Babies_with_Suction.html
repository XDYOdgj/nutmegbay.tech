Andy Bear Silicone Plates: Safe, Durable, and Fun for Baby Mealtime

Designed with safety and convenience in mind, Andy Bear silicone plates are made from 100% food-grade silicone, free from harmful materials like BPA, BPS, PVC, and lead. These soft and long-lasting plates are the perfect choice to help babies and toddlers learn to feed themselves with confidence and ease. The built-in suction plate keeps everything securely in place during meals, while the chewable spoons and toy tethers add an interactive and playful touch to your child's eating experience.

Whether you're preparing a meal at home, traveling with your little one, or enjoying a meal out, Andy Bear silicone plates are the practical and safe solution for baby mealtime.
